Rachel Carson

An environmentalist and author of Silent Spring, a book that brought attention to the dangers of pesticides and pollution.

  • Her work helped ignite the environmental movement, including efforts to protect oceans.

Sylvia Earle

A renowned marine biologist, oceanographer, and explorer.

  • Former Chief Scientist of NOAA and founder of Mission Blue, an organization dedicated to creating marine protected areas known as "Hope Spots."

Paul Watson

Known for his direct-action approach to protecting marine life, particularly against illegal whaling and overfishing.

Jacques-Yves Cousteau

A pioneering marine biologist, explorer, and filmmaker.

  • Developed the Aqua-Lung, an early scuba device, and produced numerous documentaries that raised awareness about ocean life and conservation.

David Attenborough

A natural historian and broadcaster whose documentaries, such as Blue Planet and Our Planet, have brought the beauty and fragility of the ocean to a global audience.

  • His work has inspired many to take action in ocean conservation.

James Cameron

Filmmaker and deep-sea explorer.

  • Conducted the first solo dive to the Mariana Trench, the deepest part of the ocean, and produced documentaries that highlight the importance of ocean exploration.

Enric Sala

A marine ecologist and National Geographic Explorer-in-Residence.

  • Founder of the Pristine Seas project, which aims to protect the last wild places in the ocean through exploration, research, and policy efforts.

Jean-Michel Cousteau

He founded the Ocean Futures Society, a non-profit marine conservation and education organization.

Greta Thunberg

Although known primarily for her climate activism, Thunberg's work has indirectly contributed to ocean conservation by highlighting the impact of climate change on marine ecosystems.

  • Her advocacy has inspired a new generation to care about the health of our planet, including oceans.

Al Gore

A former U.S. Vice President and environmental advocate whose work focuses on climate change.

  • His documentary An Inconvenient Truth raised awareness about the dangers of global warming, including its impact on the oceans.

Jane Goodall

Primarily known for her work with chimpanzees, Jane Goodall has also been an advocate for ocean conservation, promoting sustainable fishing practices and marine protected areas.

  • Her work emphasizes the interconnectedness of all ecosystems, including oceans.

Leonardo DiCaprio

An actor and environmental activist who has used his platform to advocate for ocean conservation.

  • His foundation supports projects aimed at protecting marine biodiversity and addressing climate change.
